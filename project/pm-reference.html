Paper Mario Reference

This is an easy reference for what each MSBT file means, and what it contains.

Fast section links: Sticker Star, Color Splash, The Origami King

Sticker Star

Sticker Star has 46 English dialogue files. It's a little excessive, considering how little dialogue this game has — some files don't even have any text. Note that, unlike the rest of the series, Sticker Star recieved a massive retranslation in the British version, where almost every line is different in some way.

  • Enemy.msbt: Enemy names
  • global.msbt: Names of Stickers, Things, and Scraps, generic battle messages, shop dialogue, main menu, opening
  • global_event.msbt: Kersti's overworld hints
  • intermissions.msbt: Royal Sticker poems
  • porttown_harbor.msbt: Surfshine Harbor
  • SealHelp.msbt: Sticker descriptions
  • SealMuseum.msbt: Sticker Museum descriptions, song names, dialogue
  • StaffCredit.msbt: Staff credits titles and names
  • stg1_1.msbt: Warm Fuzzy Plains
  • stg1_2.msbt: Bouquet Gardens
  • stg1_3.msbt: Water's Edge Way
  • stg1_4.msbt: Hither Thither Hill
  • stg1_5.msbt: Whammino Mountain (this one has exactly one entry)
  • stg1_6.msbt: Goomba Fortress
  • stg2_1.msbt: Drybake Desert
  • stg2_2.msbt: Yoshi Sphynx
  • stg2_3.msbt: Sandshifter Ruins (no entires)
  • stg2_4.msbt: Damp Oasis
  • stg2_5.msbt: Drybake Stadium
  • stg3_1.msbt: Leaflitter Path
  • stg3_6.msbt: Wiggler's Tree House
  • stg3_10.msbt: Stump Glade, Snifit or Whiffit
  • stg3_11.msbt: Outlook Point
  • stg3_12.msbt: Whitecap Beach
  • stg3_2345789.msbt: The Bafflewood, Rustle Burrow, Tree Branch Trail
  • stg4_1.msbt: Snow Rise
  • stg4_2.msbt: Ice Flow (no entires)
  • stg4_3.msbt: The Enigmansion
  • stg4_4.msbt: Whiteout Valley
  • stg4_5.msbt: Bowser's Snow Fort
  • stg5_1.msbt: Shy Guy Jungle
  • stg5_2.msbt: Jungle Rapids
  • stg5_3.msbt: Long Fall Falls (no entires)
  • stg5_4.msbt: Chomp Ruins
  • stg5_5.msbt: Rugged Road
  • stg5_6.msbt: Rumble Volcano
  • stg6_1.msbt: Gate Cliff
  • stg6_2.msbt: Bowser Jr.'s Flotilla
  • stg6_3.msbt: Bowser's Sky Castle
  • town_epilogue.msbt: Decalburg epilogue (no entires)
  • town_feast.msbt: Decalburg (Sticker Fest grounds)
  • town_houses.msbt: Decalburg (house area)
  • town_other.msbt: Decalburg (Sling-a-Thing area)

  • town_square.msbt: Decalburg (main area)

The Origami King

The Origami King has 59 English dialogue files (not including msg.msbp). Note that the absence of W3C2 is not a mistake; there is no area that goes by this code in-game.

  • announce.msbt: Announcements that appear on the pause menu
  • battle.msbt: Battle UI, warnings, hints
  • battle_Attack.msbt: Enemy attacks in battle
  • battle_Audience.msbt: Toad audience lines in battle
  • battle_Tutorial.msbt: Tutorial battles in Peach's Castle, Whispering Woods, and Picnic Road
  • battle_W1C3.msbt: Earth Vellumental boss battle
  • battle_W1C4.msbt: Colored Pencils boss battle
  • battle_W2C2.msbt: Water Vellumental boss battle
  • battle_W2C5.msbt: Rubber Band boss battle
  • battle_W3C3.msbt: Fire Vellumental boss battle
  • battle_W3C4.msbt: Hole Punch boss battle
  • battle_W4C2.msbt: Ice Vellumental boss battle
  • battle_W4C3.msbt: Tape boss battle
  • battle_W5C3.msbt: Handaconda and Scissors boss battles
  • battle_W5G1.msbt: Boss Sumo Bro boss battle
  • battle_W6C2.msbt: Stapler and King Olly boss battles
  • bench.msbt: Dialogue from the characters when sitting on benches
  • global.msbt: Common text, including warnings, button prompts, shop dialogue, fishing, and boss names
  • glossary.msbt: Terminology including names of locations, characters, bosses, items
  • hint.msbt: Olivia's overworld hints
  • item.msbt: Item descriptions and Collectible Treasure names
  • musuem.msbt: Musee Champignon descriptions and folded Toad names
  • museum_art.msbt: Musee Champignon art gallery names
  • museum_sound.msbt: Musee Champignon sound gallery names
  • staffcredit.msbt: Staff credits titles and names
  • ui.msbt: Pause menu, control popups, maps, options, Battle Lab menu
  • W0C1.msbt: Game intro, Peach's Castle, start of the ending
  • W1C1.msbt: Whispering Woods
  • W1C2.msbt: Graffiti Underground
  • W1C3.msbt: Earth Vellumental Temple
  • W1C4.msbt: Overlook Tower
  • W1G1.msbt: Toad Town
  • W1G2.msbt: Picnic Road
  • W1G3.msbt: Overlook Mountain, Bobby tram scene
  • W2C1.msbt: Chestnut Valley
  • W2C2.msbt: Water Vellumental Shrine
  • W2C3.msbt: Eddy River
  • W2C4.msbt: Ninja Attraction / House of Tricky Ninjas
  • W2C5.msbt: Big Sho' Theater
  • W2G1.msbt: Autumn Mountain
  • W2G2.msbt: Shogun Studios
  • W3C1.msbt: Breezy Tunnel
  • W3C3.msbt: Fire Vellumental Cave
  • W3C4.msbt: Temple of Shrooms
  • W3G1.msbt: Sweetpaper Valley
  • W3G2.msbt: Scorching Sandpaper Desert (plus Minor, East, West, Far East, Far West, and Shroom City / Snif City)
  • W4C1.msbt: The Princess Peach
  • W4C2.msbt: Ice Vellumental Mountain
  • W4C3.msbt: Sea Tower
  • W4G1.msbt: The Great Sea and islands (Spade, Club, Heart, Scuffle, Mushroom, Bonehead, ?, Crescent Moon / Full Moon)

  • W5C1.msbt: Spring of Rainbows, Shy Guys Finish Last
